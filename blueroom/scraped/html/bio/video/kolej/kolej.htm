kolej - rail project

"Rail Project” is a kind of performance which arised thanks to combination of live electronic music (based on natural sounds) and visualizations – films that are being created directly during the show.
To prepare this composition Z^2 group has recorded lots of sounds connected with trains, engines and other objects or places related to the rail.
The aim of this project is to perform specific sonic and visual space throughout the live concert illustrated with images, and to create author’s interpretation of the phenomenon which the rail undoubtedly is.
During the performance you can hear and see another side of the rail – workers putting in motion engines, workshops, locomotive sheds, tractions, signallings, tunnels and viaducts.
The story is leading spectators through distances of stations, platforms, signal towers, booking-offices, waiting-rooms or interiors of trains. Participants of the spectacle have an opportunity to discharge a kind of special walk across the places related to the rail.

All sound and pictures used in the spectacle have been recorded by Z^2 group just for this project.

Z^2 group [Z square] members:

Marek Zygmunt & Krzysztof Tomaszewski/Zambari – laptop computers, samplers, midi instruments

Jacek Tumoszuk/Hijack – pictures (co-operation in the Rail project)

First demonstration of the “Rail Project” took place on the 20th of March 2005 in Plama - Creators’ Club in Gdańsk/Poland. (coauthor of the annual International Street & Open Air Theatre Festival FETA in Gdańsk)
