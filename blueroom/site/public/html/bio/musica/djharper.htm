Harper (Marcin Hubert)
First of all a music lover. Journalist writing for many Polish magazines for ten years, at present for Aktivist, Klan. He hosted a popular radio show called Funkadelia on Radiostacja and worked for Redbull Music Academy project.
In 2005 Marcin released a compilation " I Feel Good" which will soon turn into a series. Before, in 1999 he released a precursory " Plastikfikation Beat 1".
As a dj Harper specializes in a very dynamic and highly explosive fusion of funk, break beat, and house with rock energy. As a member of BEats Friendly he presents his second speciality: black grooves, classic & mellow hip hop, raga, dancehall and funk.
He used to play with a band Futro and still collaborates with Molly (chill-beats project, ex- Waxfood). Together with Bert they set up the first and so far the best known dj duo in Poland, Boogie Mafia .
In his colourful djing career Harper has played with many international stars, for example:
Chemical Brothers, UNKLE, Basement Jaxx, Plump DJs, Arrested Developement, Lee "Scratch" Perry, Freestylers, Santon Warriors, Dub Pistols, Tony Vegas, Meat Katie, Lee Coombs, Audio Bullys, Adam Freeland, Chicken Lips, Soul Of Man, Dreadzone, Krafty Kuts, Etienne De Crecy, DJ Krust (Reprazent), Cut La Roc, Morcheeba DJ's and more.
He also played with many Polish artists, such as: Fisz, Tworzywo Sztuczne, Sistars, Cinq G, Masala Sound System, Zjednoczenie Sound System.
Marcin was part of the jury of prestigious Heineken Music Thirst, he took part in Miller Fresh project, played at Red Bull King Of Air and Heineken Open'er Festival, Smirnoff Experience and many other important events.
