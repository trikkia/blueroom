TOBIAS THOMAS

Llives and works in Cologne since 1992. Free writer since 1994, then editor-in-chief since 1995 for HOUSE ATTACK, a magazin for elektronic music, art, poetry and more (not existing anymore). Free contributor for SPEX Magazine and Frontpage since 1993, for GROOVE Magazinesince 1994; Since january 2000 permanent editor only for SPEX Magazine.
- DJ since 1990; since 1993 more and more together with Michael Mayer.
- 1994-95 Resident DJ at Cologne’s »IZ Club« with Michael Mayer, doing a pretty successful friday night named »This is Friends Experiment«.
- since 1998 Resident-DJ for »Total Confusion« at Studio 672/Cologne, together with Michael Mayer, later on also with Superpitcher. In July 2004 »Total Confusion« had its 6th anniversary.
- since 2003 three-monthly »total confusion« residency at Robert Johnson Club, Offenbach
- since 2004 residency at Tanzhalle St. Pauli (Hamburg) and Low Club (Madrid)

INTERNATIONAL APPEARANCES IN: Istanbul, Athens, New York, London, Cleveland, Kyoto, Rotterdam, Geneva, Bruxelles, Barcelona, Gent, Amsterdam, Kopenhagen, Zürich, Bern, Malmö, Lüttich, Nikosia, Benicassim Festival Spain, Turin, Bologna, Vienna, Bern, Beirut, Nice, Aman, Paris, Porto, Lisboa, Vigo, Tokyo, Buenos Aires, Lausanne, Madrid
