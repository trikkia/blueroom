dexter - discografia

Other project names he has recorded under include : The Noodle project .. Berserk.. Cornflake Junkie..Remy Verheijen.. and more.

2000..Started Klakson Records together with Steven de Peven and Steffi to create a label to get away from more serious 'purist' styles of electro. First 2 releases "I Dont Care" and "Intruder" were by Dexter and instantly became hits and were quickly licensed on all the biggest electro compilations.

Just released 3rd Dexter release called "RAW!"

2005: Producing the Rotterdam band called Aplle Garden/ working on Dexter Album and other projects...
